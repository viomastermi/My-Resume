Education

  • The Beginning of Knowledge : Quran Memorization & Islamic Education In 2002 | Mu'izzuddin Ismail embarked on a sacred path of knowledge by enrolling in an intensive Quran memorization program (Tahfiz). Within just two years, he successfully completed the memorization of all 30 Juz of the Holy Quran, from Surah al-Fatihah to Surah an-Naas, a milestone completed with full dedication and spiritual commitment by July 2004.
  • Early Academic ExcellenceIn 2006 | he sat for the Selangor Religious Secondary Certificate (SMA) examination, where he achieved outstanding results, ranking among the top-performing students in Islamic education at the secondary level.
  • Studies in Yemen: Islamic Medicine & Cupping Therapy | Between 2008 and 2011, Mu'izzuddin Ismail pursued further education in Yemen, where he deepened his knowledge of traditional Islamic healing, including cupping therapy (bekam) and ruqyah syariah. During his time there, he gained extensive hands-on experience in treating patients through both spiritual and physical healing methods.
  • Spiritual Exploration & the Path of Tasawwuf | Between 2013 and 2015, Mu'izzuddin Ismail devoted himself to the study of Tasawwuf (Islamic spirituality) and metaphysical theology through the Tariqat al-Ghazaliyyah (Malaysia) and Tariqat An-Naqsyabandiyyah. His journey then continued to Egypt, where he studied under world-renowned Hadith scholars, Sheikh Muhammad Sa'id Mamduh and Sheikh Ahmad Muhammad An-Nahrity, delving into Hadith sciences, Quranic studies, and Islamic healing. While in Egypt, he also embraced a third spiritual order, the Tariqat As-Syazuliyyah, deepening his practice of inner purification and spiritual discipline.
  • Study of Metaphysical Healing & Islamic Energy Sciences | Throughout 2016–2017, he further explored a range of metaphysical healing arts, including Quranic ruqyah, spiritual energy work, aura sciences, and Islamic-based internal force techniques (jurus) all based on authentic sources and traditional wisdom.
  • Transition to Technology & Software Engineering | Since 2019, Mu'izzuddin Ismail has immersed himself in the world of technology and software development, studying computer science, software engineering, and gaining proficiency as a Full-Stack Developer. He has since ventured into web and mobile app development, focusing on AI-driven platforms, automation systems, and digital product ecosystems.
